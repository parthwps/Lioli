Stone-Look Porcelain Stoneware

Stone-look porcelain stoneware brings the inherent beauty of stone into our homes. The combination of advanced technology and authenticity makes them indistinguishable from natural quarry material. Choosing tile for the interior and exterior is like simultaneously giving your space a face-lift and a new soul.

Porcelain tiles that look like stone offer a very realistic appearance of stone, like typical veins, and grainy and irregular texture, thanks to advanced technology that make them perfect for every space. The roughness, grains, veins and shades of each layer are properly specified to give stone-effect tiles all the stone's inherent beauty.

Indeed, the technical characteristic of porcelain tile provides an unparalleled level of resistance to impact, wear, weather, and chemical damage, making it a rock-solid covering in the truest sense. Thus, porcelain stone-look tiles capture the charm of stone flooring shaped by time in an innovative and modern way.

The Advantages Of Using Stone-Look Porcelain Stoneware Tiles

Here, we bring you some of the stunning features of stone-look porcelain tiles, making them a preferred choice over natural stone flooring.

Let’s see!

01. Aesthetic Appearance

Stone-look porcelain tiles are more fabulous and gorgeous than any natural stone, and stunning stone-effect tiles look incredibly realistic. Advanced technology makes it possible to achieve small details of natural stone - the veins, roughness, and irregular surface found in marble or granite to create porcelain tile that looks as beautiful as stone.

These features add to the stone's aesthetic and make each tile unique with its natural variation.

02. Durability

Natural stones easily get scratches and stains; thus, using them in high-footfall areas will not take long to get worn out. On the other hand, porcelain tiles are man-made. These tiles are consistent and extremely durable than natural stone. They are increasingly designed to intimate natural stone effects with the durability of porcelain tile.

03. Easy To Maintain

Natural stones like marble or travertine require a lot of upkeep and maintenance like sealing, polishing and cleaning to keep them looking their best. In comparison, stone-effect porcelain tile requires very little upkeep than natural stone.

TIP: Simply mop them with lukewarm water and a cleaning agent twice a week. They are easy to maintain and will maintain their beautiful appearance for a long time.

Read to know how to clean porcelain tile with do’s and don’ts.

04. Cost Effective

Natural stone flooring is a timeless beauty and elegance and adds immersive value to space. However, natural stone can be costly for the interior and also has a high-maintenance cost.

Porcelain tile that looks like stone is a much more cost-effective choice with easy maintenance, durability and aesthetic look.

05. Eco-Friendly

The process of natural stone extraction is long and complicated and leaves negative environmental impacts. On the other hand, stone look-alike porcelain tiles are manufactured with clay and other minerals in state-of-art-technology in the most eco-friendly way.

06. Easy To Install

Natural stone expense extends to the installation phase too. It always requires a professional to install them. Installing stone-effect porcelain tile is a bit challenging because of its hard nature material. It requires a little bit of skill and the right tool to install.

Factors To Consider While Choosing Stone Look Porcelain Stoneware Tile

It's important to choose the perfect tile for your home. So here are some factors to consider while choosing a stone-look porcelain floor tile.

01. Right Type Of Stone Tile

As mentioned, different types of porcelain tiles are better for different spaces. Each type of tile has its own set of advantages and disadvantages in damp areas. Consider where you will install your stone-look porcelain floor tile and see what damage it will face. Find the right type of stone porcelain tile material that balances the required strength and desired look.

02. Style

Stone-look porcelain tile offers the authentic style of each stone material, is long-lasting and easy to clean. The elegant & luxurious look of marble stone, rustic matt & neutral yet contemporary stone tile adds a sense of luxury to your setting.

03. Shade

The shade of tile is also important when choosing stone tile as it affects the aesthetic immensely. For example, grey shade is popular for refined & elegant, black is associated with luxury & sophistication, and white emotes comfort & peace.

04. Seamless look

As you know, stone look porcelain tile is available in various formats and designs. If you are looking for a seamless and impeccable look, then large-format porcelain tiles are the ideal choice. Large format tile has narrower joints than usual to help to create an undefined and flawless look. Installation of large format porcelain tile must be difficult, but the required skill and DIY tricks assist you in achieving the best result.

Note:- Always choose brand porcelain tile for better durability and more aesthetic features.
